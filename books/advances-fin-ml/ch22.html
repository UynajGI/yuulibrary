    回测的危险#

    11.1 动机#

    回测是量化武器库中最关键但最不被理解的技术之一。一个常见的误解是认为 of backtesting as a research tool. 研究和回测就像酒驾。不要在回测的影响下做研究。 大多数发表的回测 in journals are flawed, as the result of selection bias on multiple tests (Bailey, Borwein, López de Prado, and Zhu [2014]；Harvey 等 [2016]）。可以写一整本书 listing all the different errors people make while backtesting. I may be the academic author with the largest number of journal articles on backtesting^\ [1]^ and investment performance metrics, and still I do not feel I would have the stamina to compile all the different errors I have seen over the past 20 years. This chapter is not a crash course on backtesting, but a short list of some of the common errors that even seasoned professionals make.

    11.2 不可能的任务：完美的回测#

    在最狭义的定义中，回测是对策略在过去一段时间内运行时表现的模拟。 As such, it is a hypothetical, and by no means an experiment. At a physics laboratory, like Berkeley Lab, we can repeat an experiment while controlling for environmental variables, in order to deduce a precise cause-effect relationship. In contrast, a backtest isnotan experiment, and it does not prove anything. 回测不保证任何事，即使我们能乘时光机回到过去，也不能保证达到那个夏普比率 in our retrofitted DeLorean DMC-12 (Bailey and López de Prado [2012]). Random draws would have been different. The past would not repeat itself.

    那么回测的意义是什么？它是对若干变量的合理性检查，包括下注规模、换手率、对成本的韧性以及在给定情景下的行为。 A good backtest can be extremely helpful, but backtesting well is extremely hard. In 2014 a team of quants at Deutsche Bank, led by Yin Luo, published a study under the title "Seven Sins of Quantitative Investing" (Luo et al. [2014]). It is a very graphic and accessible piece that I would advise everyone in this business to read carefully. In it, this team mentions the usual suspects:

    1. Survivorship bias: Using as investment universe the current one, hence ignoring that some companies went bankrupt and securities were delisted along the way.
    2. Look-ahead bias: Using information that was not public at the moment the simulated decision would have been made. Be certain about the timestamp for each data point. Take into account release dates, distribution delays, and backfill corrections.
    3. Storytelling: Making up a story ex-post to justify some random pattern.
    4. Data mining and data snooping: Training the model on the testing set.
    5. Transaction costs: Simulating transaction costs is hard because the only way to be certain about that cost would have been to interact with the trading book (i.e., to do the actual trade).
    6. Outliers: Basing a strategy on a few extreme outcomes that may never happen again as observed in the past.
    7. Shorting: Taking a short position on cash products requires finding a lender. The cost of lending and the amount available is generally unknown, and depends on relations, inventory, relative demand, etc.

    这些只是大多数期刊发表论文经常犯的一些基本错误。 Other common errors include computing performance using a non-standard method (第 14 章); ignoring hidden risks; focusing only on returns while ignoring other metrics; confusing correlation with causation; selecting an unrepresentative time period; failing to expect the unexpected; ignoring the existence of stop-out limits or margin calls; ignoring funding costs; and forgetting practical aspects (Sarfati [2015]). There are many more, but really, there is no point in listing them, because of the title of the next section.

    11.3 即使你的回测完美，它可能也是错的#

    恭喜！你的回测完美无瑕——每个人都能复现你的结果，你的假设如此保守，以至于连你的老板都无法反对。 You have paid for every trade more than double what anyone could possibly ask. You have executed hours after the information was known by half the globe, at a ridiculously low volume participation rate. Despite all these egregious costs, your backtest still makes a lot of money. Yet, this flawless backtest is probably wrong. Why? Because only an expert can produce a flawless backtest. Becoming an expert means that you have run tens of thousands of backtests over the years. In conclusion, this is not the first backtest you produce, so we need to account for the possibility that this is a false discovery, a statistical fluke that inevitably comes up after you run multiple tests on the same dataset.

    回测令人抓狂的是，你越擅长它，错误发现出现的可能性越大。 Beginners fall for the seven sins of Luo et al. [2014] (there are more, but who's counting?). Professionals may produce flawless backtests, and will still fall for multiple testing, selection bias, or backtest overfitting (Bailey and López de Prado [2014b]).

    11.4 回测不是研究工具#

    第 8 章讨论了替代效应、联合效应、掩蔽、MDI、 MDA、SFI、并行特征、堆叠特征等。 Even if some features are very important, it does not mean that they can be monetized through an investment strategy. Conversely, there are plenty of 即使基于无关特征也会看起来有利可图的策略。特征重要性是真正的研究工具，因为它帮助我们理解 ML 算法发现的模式的本质，无论其货币化如何。 Critically, feature importance is derived] ex-ante [, before the historical performance is simulated.

    相比之下，回测不是研究工具。它几乎不能让我们洞悉某个策略为什么会赚钱的原因。 Just as a lottery winner may feel he has done something to deserve his luck, there is always some] ex-post story (Luo's sin number three). Authors claim to have found hundreds of "alphas" and "factors," and there is always some convoluted explanation for them. Instead, what they have found are the lottery tickets that won the last game. The winner has cashed out, and those numbers are useless for the next round. If you would not pay extra for those lottery tickets, why would you care about those hundreds of alphas? Those authors never tell us about all the tickets that were sold, that is, the millions of simulations it took to find these "lucky" alphas.

    回测的目的是丢弃糟糕的模型，而不是改进它们。 Adjusting your model based on the backtest results is a waste of time . . . and it's dangerous. Invest your time and effort in getting all the components right, as we've discussed elsewhere in the book: structured data, labeling, weighting, ensembles, cross-validation, feature importance, bet sizing, etc. By the time you are backtesting, it is too late. 在模型完全确定之前永远不要回测。如果回测失败，从头开始。 If you do that, the chances of finding a false discovery will drop substantially, but still they will not be zero.

    11.5 一些通用建议#

    回测过拟合可以定义为多次回测上的选择偏差。 Backtest overfitting takes place when a strategy is developed to perform well on a backtest, by monetizing random historical patterns. 由于那些随机模式不太可能在未来再次出现，如此开发的策略将会失败。 Every backtested strategy is overfit to some extent as a result of "selection bias": The only backtests that most people share are those that portray supposedly winning investment strategies.

    如何解决回测过拟合可以说是量化金融中最根本的问题。 Why? Because if there was an easy answer to this question, investment firms would achieve high performance with certainty, as they would invest only in winning backtests. Journals would assess with confidence whether a strategy may be a false positive. 金融可以成为 Popper 和 Lakatos 意义上的真正科学 (López de Prado [2017]). What makes backtest overfitting so hard to assess is that the probability of false positives changes with every new test conducted on the same dataset, and that information is either unknown by the researcher or not shared with investors or referees. 虽然没有简单的方法防止过拟合，但一些步骤可以帮助减少其存在。

    1. 为整个资产类别或投资宇宙开发模型，而非为特定证券（第 8 章）。投资者分散投资，因此他们不会只在证券 Y 上犯错误 X。如果你只在证券 Y 上发现错误 X，无论看起来多有利可图，它很可能是错误发现。
    2. 应用 bagging（第 6 章）作为既防止过拟合又减少预测误差方差的手段。如果 bagging 恶化了策略的表现，它很可能过拟合了少量观测或异常值。
    3. 在所有研究完成（第 1-10 章）之前不要回测。
    4. 记录在数据集上进行的每次回测，以便在最终选择的结果上估计回测过拟合的概率（见 Bailey、Borwein、López de Prado 和 Zhu [2017a] 及第 14 章），夏普比率可以根据进行的试验次数正确 deflate（Bailey 和 López de Prado [2014b]）。
    5. 模拟情景而非历史（第 12 章）。标准回测是历史模拟，很容易过拟合。历史只是实现的随机路径，它可能完全不同。你的策略应该在广泛的情景下都有利可图，而非仅仅在轶事性的历史路径上。过拟合数千个「如果」情景的结果更难。
    6. 如果回测未能识别出有利可图的策略，从头开始。抵制重用这些结果的诱惑。遵循回测第二定律。

    11.6 策略选择#

    在第 7 章中，我们讨论了标签中序列条件的存在如何使标准 k 折交叉验证失效， 因为随机采样会将冗余观测溅入训练集和测试集。我们必须找到一种不同的（真正的样本外）验证程序：一种在与用于训练模型的观测最不可能相关/冗余的观测上评估模型的程序。 See Arlot and Celisse [2010] for a survey.

    Scikit-learn 实现了走步法时间折叠方法。 ^[2]^ Under this approach, testing moves forward (in the time direction) with the goal of preventing leakage. This is consistent with the way historical backtests (and trading) are done. However, in the presence of long-range serial dependence, testing one observation away from the end of the training set may not suffice to avoid informational leakage. We will retake this point in 第 12 章, Section 12.2.

    走步法的一个缺点是它很容易过拟合。原因是，没有随机采样，只有一条测试路径可以反复重复直到出现假阳性。 Like in standard CV, some randomization is needed to avoid this sort of performance targeting or backtest optimization, while 避免与训练集相关的示例泄露到测试集中。接下来，我们将介绍一种用于策略选择的 CV 方法， based on the estimation of the probability of backtest overfitting (PBO). We leave for 第 12 章 an explanation of CV methods for backtesting.

    2. [An analyst develops an ML algorithm where he generates a signal

      using closing prices, and executed at close. What's the sin?

    3. [There is a 98.51% correlation between total revenue generated by

      arcades and computer science doctorates awarded in the United States. As the number of doctorates is expected to grow, should we invest in arcades companies? If not, what's the sin?
