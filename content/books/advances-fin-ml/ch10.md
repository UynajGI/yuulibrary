# 下注规模

## 10.1 动机
策略游戏与投资之间有着迷人的相似之处。我合作过的一些最优秀的投资组合经理是出色的
poker players, perhaps more so than chess players. One reason is bet
sizing, for which Texas Hold\'em provides a great analogue and training
ground. Your ML algorithm can achieve high accuracy, but if you do not
size your bets properly, your investment strategy will inevitably lose
money. In this chapter we will review a few approaches to size bets from
ML predictions.
